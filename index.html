About me

I am a software engineer from Portland, Oregon. I received my Bachelors of Computer Science from Oregon State University with a concentration in Computer Systems. I have interned at Intel and Elemental Technologies. Recently, I developed and designed a prototype version of an accounting tool targeting farmers called AgBiz Logic for the Oregon State University Applied Economic Department. The website accomplished on attracting around 12 growers from the Oregon and Washington area to sign up.

Currently I am contractor at Intel as a Software Engineer where I am working with Source Control Management and Jenkins CI. I am very proactive in finding bottlenecks and ways to further automate manual tasks in my current position. My current roles have me wearing multiple hats, where I improve our Jenkins build systems or resolve Bluetooth Firmware/Software Engineers daily issues with their builds. My current goals is to gain more experience in the DevOps role and start using Puppet Lab/Chef.
